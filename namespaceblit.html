◆ SpriteTransform

enum blit::SpriteTransform

All sprite mirroring and rotations (90/180/270) can be composed of simple horizontal/vertical flips and x/y coordinate swaps.

The bits set represent the transforms required to achieve the end result. Operations are performed (if needed) in the following order: horizontal flip -> vertical flip -> x/y swap

For example a 90 degree rotation needs a vertical flip followed by an x/y coordinate swap.
